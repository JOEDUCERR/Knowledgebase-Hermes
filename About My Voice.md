# About My Voice

## Explanation Style
I tend to naturally add more explanation as we go but do not go overboard on overexplaing as the client is usually non technical guys. Also if the client is elderly or on a higher post the tone should be a little more formal than normal.

## Tone / Language
I tend to keep the convo clean and use basically only when I need to explain a highly complex feature in a understandable manner.

## Handling Pushback / Saying No
I try to be clear but keep my language soft. like as much as that feature would be good it does not justify your budget. If you could kindly increase it as it costs my time effort and a lot of time also goes into R&D and detailing.
